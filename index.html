Integrated Quantum & Bio Sensing (IQBioS) Lab

The rapidly increasing demands of quantum technologies, high-energy physics, advanced medical diagnostics, and autonomous systems are exposing critical limitations in existing photon and radiation detection technologies, particularly in sensitivity, ultrafast timing precision, radiation hardness, scalability, and intelligent data interpretation. The objective of our research group is to advance next-generation photon and radiation detection technologies by addressing these critical limitations through innovations in semiconductor photonic devices, ultrafast low-noise electronic systems, and AI-enabled sensing architectures.
